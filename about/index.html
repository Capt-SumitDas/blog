Who I Am

Hey! I'm a passionate cybersecurity researcher with a focus on offensive security, web application penetration testing, and CTF competitions. I started my journey into security after getting curious about how websites could be broken — and I never stopped.

I actively participate in CTF competitions through platforms like HackTheBox, TryHackMe, and picoCTF. I also do bug bounty hunting on Bugcrowd and HackerOne, where I've responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities in several web applications.

This blog is my way of giving back to the security community. Every writeup, tutorial, and research note here is designed to help the next person on the same path.
